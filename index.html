Grand Cherokee

The Jeep® Grand Cherokee makes it mark with a unique appearance that combines Jeep exclusive 20-inch high-gloss black alloy wheels with body colour fascias, wheel arch flares and side sills.

Jeep’s 7-slot grille is adorned with gloss black surrounds and platinum chrome vents up front, with dual bright exhaust tips down back to complete aggressive chrome and black out look.

A distinctive flair is added to the Grand Cherokee interior with black Capri leather and perforated suede seat upholstery wrapping around standard heated front seats.
